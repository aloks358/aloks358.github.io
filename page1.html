Welcome to ABCD!

ABCD Dance Company is Dubai’s premier Indian dance company, specializing in artistic and authentic choreography. The company was taken over by Rithesh Penha and Silvy Penha with the purpose of sharing Indian dance in all its forms – folk, classical, and film – with a global and diverse audience. The ABCD Ensemble and teaching staff are trained in a broad range of dance styles and proud of themselves on offering stunning performances and highly skilled instruction. ABCD signature style is elegant, high-energy, and blends the excitement of salsa and hip hop with the grace and technique of Bollywood dance.

ABCD offers classes in a number of styles for dancers of all levels.

  • Bollywood
  • Hip-Hop
  • Jazz
  • Contemporary
  • Freestyle
  • Bhangra
  • Classical Indian
  • Gujarati Folk Dance
  • Fitness
  • Zumba
  • Gymnastics
  • Yoga Movement
